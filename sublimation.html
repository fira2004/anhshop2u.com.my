Sublimation Printing

Sublimation printing is a technique that uses heat to transfer dye directly into materials such as polyester fabrics or specially coated surfaces. The ink becomes part of the material rather than sitting on top of it, resulting in a smooth and long-lasting print.

This method is ideal for full-color designs and all-over prints, making it a popular choice for sportswear and customized merchandise.

Common applications include:

  • Sports jerseys
  • Polyester T-shirts
  • Mugs and drinkware
  • Mouse pads
  • Lanyards
  • Promotional items

Sublimation prints are known for their vibrant colors, durability, and smooth finish that will not easily crack or peel.
