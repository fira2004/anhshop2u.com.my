About Us

ANH Smart Venture Sdn Bhd is a Malaysian-based company specializing in eco-conscious footwear solutions. The company focuses on producing high-quality rubber slippers designed for comfort, travel, and everyday use.

Company Information

Company Overview

Established with the goal of delivering modern, stylish, and sustainable footwear solutions. We combine quality craftsmanship with innovative design to stay competitive.

Target Market

We serve local and international customers, including students, professionals, and retailers seeking comfort and customization.

Business Type

Retail and product customisation specialising in high-quality slippers with personalised sizes, colours, and design options.
