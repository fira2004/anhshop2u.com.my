Direct-To-Garment

Direct-to-Garment (DTG) printing is a modern digital printing method that prints designs directly onto fabric using specialized textile inks. This technology allows for highly detailed designs, gradient colors, and photo-quality prints, making it ideal for artwork with complex graphics.

Best used for:

  • Custom T-shirts
  • Fashion brand merchandise
  • Detailed illustrations
  • Photographic prints on apparel

DTG printing produces soft-feel prints with excellent color accuracy, making it a popular choice for customized clothing and small production runs.
