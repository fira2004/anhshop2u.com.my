UV Printing

UV printing is an advanced printing technology that allows designs to be printed directly onto various materials with high precision. The ink is instantly cured using ultraviolet light, creating prints that are scratch-resistant, vibrant, and long-lasting. This technology enables printing on surfaces that are not typically suitable for traditional printing methods.

Suitable materials include:

•Acrylic products

•Phone cases

•Keychains

•Plastic items

•Metal surfaces

•Signage and display items

UV printing is often used for corporate gifts, branded merchandise, souvenirs, and customized promotional items.

ANH Smart Venture offers UV printing services for promotional items, signage, and custom products, ensuring a professional finish every time.
